Project Motivation: to develop effective methodologies for detecting cyberbullying in social media platforms. By leveraging machine learning algorithms and NLP techniques, I aim to classify and identify harmful interactions and toxic behavior across different social media networks. Additionally, my research incorporates data science principles to analyze and process large-scale social media data, enabling more accurate and efficient detection models. This work seeks to contribute to a safer and more supportive online environment through automated systems for real-time monitoring and intervention.
